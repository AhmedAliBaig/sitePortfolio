Getting Started

Welcome to PMCL

PMCL is a service that allows you to securely store credit cards and use them to transact against any number of payment gateways and third party APIs. It does this by simultaneously providing a card tokenization/vault service as well as a gateway and receiver integration service.

By using tokenization, PMCL is able to execute against any number of gateways and APIs on your behalf without the credit card data ever touching your servers.

Quick Start

In order to start executing transactions and collecting payments from your users, you will need to:

  1. Register for a free account, and then create your API credentials.
  2. Add a gateway to transact against (you can also transact against non-gateway third party APIs)
  3. Start collecting payment information from your users
  4. Make a purchase against the gateway with your users’ payment information

If you’re interested in transacting against a third party API, and not a gateway, please see our Payment Method Distribution functionality.
